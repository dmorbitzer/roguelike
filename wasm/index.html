Welcome to my small roguelike game written in RUST

Move with WASD and attack an enemies by running into them

Pick up Items with G

Open inventory with I and drop menu with N

Current Features:

  • Random map generation
  • Movement with WASD
  • Melee Combat by running into enemies
  • Enemy AI trying to get to the player and attack
  • Tooltips on hover over enemies
  • Inventory System
  • Drop System
