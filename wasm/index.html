Welcome to my small roguelike game written in RUST

Controls

Move with WASD and attack enemies by running into them

Pick up Items with G

Open inventory with I and drop menu with N

Use a magic scroll by left clicking

Items

Health Potion: Heals the player

Magic Missile Scroll: Throw a magic missile at a single target

Fireball Scroll: Throw a huge fireball at multiple targets

Confusion Scroll: Confuse a single target

Feature List

  • Random map generation
  • Movement with WASD
  • Melee Combat by running into enemies
  • Enemy AI trying to get to the player and attack
  • Tooltips on hover over enemies
  • Inventory System
  • Drop System
  • Magic Scroll System
  • Save and Load System (only Desktop)
